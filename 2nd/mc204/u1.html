    Marketing ek business term hai jise "vyapar" ya "bazaarikaran" kehte hain. Yeh ek samuhik prakriya hai jisme vyapari ya kampni apne utpadon aur services ko khareedne ke liye grahakon tak pahunchane ki koshish karta hai.

    Marketing ka arth hai grahakon ke needs aur unki samasyaon ko samajhna aur uske anusar utpadon aur services ko pradan karna. Iska swaroop bahut vyapak hai aur isme grahakon ki demand ko purn karna, unke expectation ko samjhna, aur unki satisfaction ko badhana shamil hai.

    Marketing ka mahatva bahut bada hai. Ye ek kampni ke vikas aur safalta ke liye avashyak hai. Marketing ke through vyapari grahakon tak apne utpadon ka samarthan karta hai aur unhe samjhata hai ki kyun unke utpadon aur services ka upyog karna chahiye. Isse vyapari apne competitors se alag hota hai aur grahakon ki taraf se vishvas aur loyalty prapt karta hai.

    Marketing ka scope bhi bahut vistrit hai. Yeh samajik, arthik aur praudyogiki sambandhit kshetron ko include karta hai. Marketing mein utpadon ke prachar, pricing, distribution channels, promotion aur sales activities jaise kai pahlu hote hain. Isme market research, target audience identification, advertising, public relations, aur brand management bhi shamil hai.

    Marketing ki sahi planning aur execution ke dwara vyapari apne grahakon ke behtar samajh aur unke tarah utpadon ko utpann kar sakta hai. Isse grahakon ko sahi samay par sahi utpad aur services milte hain aur vyapari ki safalta bhi badhti hai.

    Samanya roop se kaha jaye toh marketing grahakon aur vyapariyon ke beech ek sandarbh aur sankalp ki sthapna hai. Iska uddeshya hai vyapari ki jaruraton aur grahak ki zarooraton ko mel karke samriddhi ki sthapna karna.

    Marketing ka vikas ya "evolution" production orientation philosophy, sales orientation philosophy, customer orientation philosophy, aur social orientation philosophy ke saath juda hua hai. Yahan takneek aur drishtikon ke traditional view aur modern view bhi hai.

    1. Production Orientation Philosophy:
    Production orientation philosophy ek traditional view hai, jisme vyapari utpadon ki prabandhan aur utpadan par adhik dhyan dete hain. Is drishtikon mein, vyapari maante hain ki agar unhone acchi quality ke utpadon ko prastut kiya aur unhe samay par market mein uplabdh kiya, to grahak swabhavik roop se unhe khareedenge. Yahan vyapari apne production capabilities aur efficiency par focus karte hain.

    2. Sales Orientation Philosophy:
    Sales orientation philosophy bhi traditional view hai, jisme vyapari utpadon ke bechne par adhik dhyan dete hain. Is drishtikon mein, vyapari maante hain ki agar unhone utpadon ko prachar aur bechne ke liye sahi marketing techniques aur selling skills ka istemal kiya, to grahak khud-ba-khud unhe khareedenge. Yahan vyapari apne bechne ki prakriya par focus karte hain.

    3. Customer Orientation Philosophy:
    Customer orientation philosophy modern view hai, jisme vyapari grahakon ke needs aur preferences ko samajhne aur unki puri satisfaction ko prapt karne par adhik dhyan dete hain. Is drishtikon mein, vyapari maante hain ki agar ve grahakon ke requirements ko samjhte hain aur unhe sahi value aur utpad pradan karte hain, to grahak unhe khareedne ke liye pravrit honge. Yahan vyapari grahakon ko samman dete hain aur long-term customer relationships ko build karne par focus karte hain.

    4. Social Orientation Philosophy:
    Social orientation philosophy bhi modern view hai, jisme vyapari apne vyavasayik karobar ke sath-sath samajik jimmedariyon ko bhi samjhte hain. Is drishtikon mein, vyapari maante hain ki unka vyavasayik karobar samajik aur paryavaran suvidhaon ko dhyan mein rakhkar chalna chahiye. Yahan vyapari samajik hit ke liye samajik karyakramon mein bhaag lete hain aur samajik jimmedariyon ka palan karte hain.


    Traditional View:
    Traditional view mein marketing ka dhyan utpadon ki prabandhan, bechne aur samarpan par adhik hota hai. Isme grahakon ke preferences aur needs ko samajhne par kam dhyan diya jata hai.

    Modern View:
    Modern view mein marketing ka dhyan grahakon ke needs, preferences, aur satisfaction par adhik hota hai. Vyapari grahakon ke sath engagement, customer value, aur long-term relationships ko important samajhte hain. Isme samajik aur paryavaran suvidhaon ka dhyan rakhna bhi mahatvapurna hai. Is tarah se, marketing ka vikas production orientation philosophy se lekar customer orientation philosophy, social orientation philosophy tak hua hai
