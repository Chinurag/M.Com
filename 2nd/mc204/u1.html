    Marketing ek business term hai jise "vyapar" ya "bazaarikaran" kehte hain. Yeh ek samuhik prakriya hai jisme vyapari ya kampni apne utpadon aur services ko khareedne ke liye grahakon tak pahunchane ki koshish karta hai. Marketing ka arth hai grahakon ke needs aur unki samasyaon ko samajhna aur uske anusar utpadon aur services ko pradan karna. Iska swaroop bahut vyapak hai aur isme grahakon ki demand ko purn karna, unke expectation ko samjhna, aur unki satisfaction ko badhana shamil hai. Marketing ka mahatva bahut bada hai. Ye ek kampni ke vikas aur safalta ke liye avashyak hai. Marketing ke through vyapari grahakon tak apne utpadon ka samarthan karta hai aur unhe samjhata hai ki kyun unke utpadon aur services ka upyog karna chahiye. Isse vyapari apne competitors se alag hota hai aur grahakon ki taraf se vishvas aur loyalty prapt karta hai. Marketing ka scope bhi bahut vistrit hai. Yeh samajik, arthik aur praudyogiki sambandhit kshetron ko include karta hai. Marketing mein utpadon ke prachar, pricing, distribution channels, promotion aur sales activities jaise kai pahlu hote hain. Isme market research, target audience identification, advertising, public relations, aur brand management bhi shamil hai. Marketing ki sahi planning aur execution ke dwara vyapari apne grahakon ke behtar samajh aur unke tarah utpadon ko utpann kar sakta hai. Isse grahakon ko sahi samay par sahi utpad aur services milte hain aur vyapari ki safalta bhi badhti hai. Samanya roop se kaha jaye toh marketing grahakon aur vyapariyon ke beech ek sandarbh aur sankalp ki sthapna hai. Iska uddeshya hai vyapari ki jaruraton aur grahak ki zarooraton ko mel karke samriddhi ki sthapna karna.
